●Who we are

We do one thing exceptionally well: place the right person with the right team.

Built for retention, not churn

Our typical placement runs over a year. We pre-screen for culture fit, not just skill. If someone doesn’t click with the client’s style, we don’t send them.

AI on the inside, humans on the front line

Every applicant’s intro video gets auto-scored for English clarity. Test projects get an AI advisory grade. A recruiter still makes the call, but the signal is real.

Our reputation is your guarantee

Every placement carries a lifetime replacement promise. If it doesn’t work out, for any reason, we restaff at no extra cost. Bad fit costs us, not you.

●What “top 1%” actually means

Four gates. Most applicants don’t clear all four.

01
AI fit + English screen

Intro video transcribed and scored by AI on clarity, fluency, accent, audio, and energy. Below an objective threshold? Auto-decline.

02
Skill assessment

A short, role-specific test project. Editors edit. Designers design. Admins work through a real-world scenario. We pay you for it.

03
Recruiter review

A human looks at every passing submission. Background, reliability signals, work-history coherence, response speed.

04
Client-fit match

We don’t shotgun candidates at clients. Every shortlist is hand-curated for the role’s exact brief. Usually 2 to 3 names, not 20.

●What it takes

The bar is real. The bar is the only thing in your way.

Conversational+ English

You’ll be on calls with English-speaking clients across multiple time zones. We score this via your intro video, not a self-rated dropdown.

Reliable internet

At least 25 Mbps down and 10 Mbps up. We run a speed test from the apply page to verify.

Dedicated workspace

A quiet place to work full-time hours. No coffee shops, no shared bedrooms during calls.

Real samples of work

Editors and designers: a portfolio link with finished work. Admin: examples from past roles count.
