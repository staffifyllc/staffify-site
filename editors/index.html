How It Works

How we find the right editor for your business

We don't just send you a portfolio and call it done. Every editor we place runs through the same rigorous process so you know exactly what you're getting, what it costs per video, and what to expect on turnaround.

STEP 01

We analyze your current workflow

Before we touch a candidate, we look at how video editing actually moves through your business today. What's your project management system? Where does footage live? Who reviews? Where are the bottlenecks? That snapshot becomes the spec we hire against.

STEP 02

Test projects on your real footage

We take your actual footage and have candidate editors complete real test projects, not generic samples. You approve the work before we move them forward. If their cut doesn't match your brand voice on real content, they don't continue. Full stop.

STEP 03

Background checks on the approved ones

Approved editors run through a traditional recruiting layer: reference checks, hardware checks, internet speed checks, and reliability testing to confirm they can hit your minimum refresh window on deliverables. If they can't ship on time, they don't make the placement list.

STEP 04

Cost per deliverable, modeled upfront

We track how long each editor's test project actually took, so by the time we recommend someone, you know not just how they perform, but the real cost-per-finished-video. No surprises after you sign. You see the unit economics before you commit.

Lifetime Replacement Guarantee

And if any of it falls short, we replace them. Free.

If the editor we place underperforms, ghosts, or just isn't the right fit for any reason, we source and place a replacement at no additional cost, no offset to your monthly rate, no cushion fees. The risk is on us, not you.
