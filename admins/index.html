How It Works

How we find the right admin for you

An admin lives inside your business. We can't just send a resume and call it done. Every Staffify executive admin runs through the same process so the person sitting in your inbox actually sounds like you, thinks like you, and protects your time the way you would.

STEP 01

We map what's actually eating your day

Before we touch a candidate, we walk through your inbox, calendar, CRM, and project tracker with you. We document the repetitive workflows, the recurring decisions, and the "quick things" that aren't quick. That becomes the spec we hire against.

STEP 02

Test tasks on your real workflows

Candidates run real test tasks on your actual systems: inbox triage, calendar coordination, a sample CRM cleanup, drafting a follow-up email in your voice. You see the work product before they ever step into the role. If it's not your voice, they don't continue.

STEP 03

Full vetting on approved candidates

Approved admins run through a traditional recruiting layer: reference checks, hardware checks, internet speed, working-hours alignment with your time zone, and communication style screening. We confirm they can show up consistently before we place them.

STEP 04

Onboarding plan modeled upfront

We track how long each test task actually took, so by the time we recommend someone, you know not just how they perform but how much of your weekly admin load they can realistically absorb. You see the load-out before you commit.

Lifetime Replacement Guarantee

If they aren't the right fit, we replace them. Free.

If the admin we place underperforms, ghosts, or just isn't the right voice in your inbox for any reason, we source and place a replacement at no additional cost, no offset to your monthly rate, no cushion fees. The risk is on us, not you.
