Our Mission

We believe the best business owners shouldn't be spending their time answering emails, editing videos, or chasing leads. That's what we're here for.

Staffify was built for the business owner who's growing fast but still doing too much themselves. We saw how hard it was to find reliable, affordable help. And how expensive and complicated it was to hire, train, and manage someone new.

So we built an infrastructure that handles all of it. You get a trained, vetted professional who shows up ready to work. We manage payroll, compliance, and HR. You just run your business.

The Founder

Paul Chareth

Founder & CEO

Paul didn't set out to build a VA company. He set out to hire one.

Running Flylisted, his Boston-based real estate marketing company operating across New England and South Florida, he kept hearing the same advice: "Just hire a VA. Delegate. Scale." So he tried. Three times. Three completely different ways.

01
The Premium Agencies
$3–5K upfront, 90–120 day guarantees. Every single VA turned over right after the guarantee window closed.
02
The Managed Model
An agency that handled everything. Until he found out his VAs were taking home 50% at best. No stability, no real commitment.
03
Going Direct
Compliance headaches, $4–5/hr roles no one could survive on. His hires were juggling five other clients just to get by.

Three attempts. Three failures. Same outcome. So Paul stopped looking for the right company. He decided to become one.

He built a system that targeted the top 1% of global talent, paid them what they were actually worth, and wrapped every placement in accountability infrastructure that made someone 5,000 miles away just as reliable as someone in the next office. That system was born inside Flylisted.

Flylisted grew into a 7-figure annual operation across 6 states. Paul stepped into a passive board role and launched Staffify to give hundreds of other business owners the same result, without the trial and error he went through to get there.

"I wasn't trying to build a company. I was trying to solve a problem I'd lived. Staffify is what happens when you refuse to accept that the options out there are good enough."
