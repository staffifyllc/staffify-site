# Client Logo Outreach — Tracking Sheet

Total active clients: **48**   ·   Currently in marquee: **17**   ·   Pending outreach: **31**

Update the **Status** column manually as you go:
- `✅ uploaded` — logo is live in the marquee at `/assets/clients/<slug>.png`
- `📨 sent` — outreach email sent, waiting for reply
- `📩 replied` — they replied with a logo, needs upload
- `❌ declined` — they said no thanks
- `⏳ pending` — haven't reached out yet

When a client sends a PNG, save it to `assets/clients/<slug>.png` and run:
```bash
python3 scripts/rebuild_marquee.py
```
