B2B email outreach

Staffify works with HVAC companies, landscaping businesses, plumbing companies, cleaning services, roofing contractors, painting companies, pest control, pool services, and virtually any home or commercial service provider. If you sell B2B services and need more qualified clients, we can build a targeted outreach campaign for you.

We handle the entire process end-to-end. First, we build a triple-verified list of ideal-fit prospects based on your Ideal Client Profile: role, industry, company size, and location. Then we write a personalized 7-email sequence tailored to your offer. Finally, we launch the campaign across up to 20 sending mailboxes over 30 days. Warm replies route directly to your inbox. You just show up to close.

Our clients average a 4.1% reply rate on targeted B2B campaigns. On a 1,500–5,000 contact campaign that translates to 60–200 replies, a meaningful portion of which are qualified buyers ready to talk. We guarantee 10 qualified meetings in 30 days, or we keep working for free until you get them.

Staffify ensures full compliance: every email includes a clear unsubscribe mechanism, accurate sender information, and honest subject lines. We contact business email addresses only, not consumer inboxes, and honor all opt-out requests immediately.
